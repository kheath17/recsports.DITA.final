Fitness Assessment

If you want information about your current fitness level before you begin training, you can take part in a fitness assessment. A fitness assessment will help you develop an exercise plan as well as identify any health problems you may encounter during exercise.

There are two different assessments:
  • The BOD POD measures your body composition, including body fat, lean mass, and metabolic rate.
    Note: If you are a student, faculty or staff member, or alumnus, the BOD POD will cost $15. Otherwise, it will cost $30.
  • The advanced fitness assessment is much more extensive and includes the BOD POD as well as measures of heart rate, blood pressure, cardiovascular fitness, muscular strength, endurance, flexibility, and mobility.
    Note: If you are a student, faculty or staff member, or alumnus, the advanced fitness assessment will cost $25. Otherwise, it will cost $50.
