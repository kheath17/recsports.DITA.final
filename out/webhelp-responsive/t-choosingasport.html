Choosing a Sport to Play

There are 26 sports available within the Intramural Sports department. Sports range from darts, dodgeball, and golf to intertube water polo. Below is a list of all the available sports. For more information regarding each individual sport, please visit <link> http://www.recsports.vt.edu/sports/intramuralsports/sports.html</link>
